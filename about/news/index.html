We would like to honour the passing away of our dear colleague and friend, Nick Diggins, on Friday 1st March 2019.  Many of you will have known Nick in a variety of mindfulness-related roles and would have found him, like us, to have been a source of inspiration, laughter, kindness, creativity and companionship.  He touched the lives of many, many people through his prolific mindfulness teaching in Sussex and we will greatly miss him. 
There are so many of us who are feeling the loss of Nick and who have been touched by his presence and his extensive mindfulness work that we felt it would be good to have an opportunity to gather together to remember Nick.

We’d like to invite you to practice together in his memory at the Sussex Education Centre, Millview hospital site, Nevill Avenue, Hove, from 6-8pm on Wednesday 13th March. We plan to practice together and have an opportunity to share memories of Nick.
If you’d like to come, please just turn up.  If you like to practice with a particular cushion etc, please bring what you need.  If you know of others who may not know about this and would like to come along , please let them know that they are very welcome.
