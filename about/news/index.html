We would like to honour the passing away of our dear colleague and friend, Nick Diggins, on Friday 1st March 2019.  Many of you will have known Nick in a variety of mindfulness-related roles and would have found him, like us, to have been a source of inspiration, laughter, kindness, creativity and companionship.  He touched the lives of many, many people through his prolific mindfulness teaching in Sussex and we will greatly miss him.
