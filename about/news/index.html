Train to teach mindfulness with Sussex Mindfulness Centre

We are running two courses in 2020 for people who would like to learn to teach mindfulness: Foundation training in mindfulness-based approaches (MBCT/MBSR) and our training programme in Adapted Mindfulness Based Interventions.

We have been involved in a process of reviewing our training pathways, making some additions and simplifying other aspects. These changes include bringing the two training cohorts together for the first 2 modules. We are pleased that this new model is enabling us to reduce the trainee fee while maintaining quality. 

Closing date for applications has been extended to 29th November 2019
