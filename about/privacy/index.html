Sussex Mindfulness Centre is committed to protecting your privacy, in accordance with the 2018 General data protection regulation (GDPR) and will not use any information we may hold about you for any purpose other than that for which it was collected.

We like to keep in touch with people who have taken an interest in training or attended mindfulness courses and events. We may ask you if you are happy to hear from us (and to add your name and email address to our mailing list). We will not share your details with anyone else. You can unsubscribe from receiving information from us at any time:

E: smc@sussexpartnership.nhs.uk
T: 0300 304 2057
This privacy policy only covers www.sussexmindfulnesscentre.nhs.uk
