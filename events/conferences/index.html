Seventh SMC Annual Conference

Venue: Sussex Education Centre, Nevill Avenue, Hove BN3 7HY
Date:   Friday 25th October 2019
Time:   9.00am-5.00pm

* Please note that the date for this conference has changed. It will now be held on Friday 25 October 2019 and not on the 23 and 24 March 2019 as previously advertised.

Featuring

  • Rebecca Crane (Director of the Centre for Mindfulness Research and Practice, Bangor University) 'What makes an effective mindfulness teacher?'
  • Bridgette O’Neill and Robert Marx (Sussex Mindfulness Centre) 'Compassionate approaches to mindfulness: Mindful Self-Compassion & Mindfulness-Based Compassionate Living'

A full programme will be available in due course.

This event is free for Sussex Partnership/SMC staff and for current/recent Sussex Partnership/SMC service users/advocates and trainers. The conference is £110 for other attendees.  Tea and coffee will be provided. 

 

We are not currently taking bookings for the 2019 conference as it is too far in advance, but you can register your interest by emailing  smc@sussexpartnership.nhs.uk
