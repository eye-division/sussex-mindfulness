8th Sussex Mindfulness Centre Annual Conference

From the cushion to the street: How can mindfulness help us respond to the world's crises?

Friends Meeting House, Ship Street, Brighton BN1 1AF
Friday 12th June 2020

Keynote Speakers:

Byron Lee: 'An Anti-oppressive Mindfulness - is a radical agenda needed to shape the future?'  

Yanai Postelnik:  'Mindfulness in the Time of Extinction: contemplating the inseparability of inner peace and outer transformation.' 

Clara Strauss & Robert Marx: 'How can we create a more mindful workplace?'

The conference is free for Sussex Mindfulness Centre advocates and for current/recent (over the last year) Sussex Partnership service users, Sussex Mindfulness Centre Trust-approved Mindfulness Teachers/ Practitioners and trainees, and Sussex Mindfulness Centre researcher staff and students. The conference is £20 for other Sussex Partnership staff and £75 for the public.
