Drop-ins and day retreats

All drop-ins and day retreats are free of charge and are open to anyone who has both completed an eight-week MBCT or MBSR group and who is either a current or ex service user, or current employee of Sussex Partnership. If this applies to you, no booking is required. Drop-ins and day retreats are held at the Sussex Education Centre on Nevill Avenue, Hove, BN3 7HZ on the Mill View hospital and Hove Polyclinic site.

Master classes and compassion-oriented teacher trainings

Events to support the development of mindfulness teachers and people interested in mindfulness and compassion-oriented approaches. SMC Annual Conference 2019

Saturday 23 March and Sunday 24 March 2019 in Hove

This will be in collaboration with other mindfulness centres in the UK who are hoping to hold parallel conferences on the same dates. The conference will coincide with an international mindfulness conference in South Africa from where we are hoping to live stream two keynote talks, including from Jon Kabat Zinn. 

The Saturday will be a day of talks and discussion, with time for networking and spending time together as a mindfulness community. The Sunday will be a day of practice.

*Please note, that we had previously advertised the conference as running from 22-26 March 2019 but this has been changed due to unforeseen changes to the funding arrangements.
