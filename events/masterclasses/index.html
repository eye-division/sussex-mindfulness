Events to support the development of MBCT teachers and people interested in mindfulness. Led by national and internationally renown speakers, as well as local mindfulness teachers with particular areas of expertise. They present cutting edge contemporary mindfulness-oriented approaches in various different contexts.

Workshops will include a mixture of teaching, discussion and experiential practice and provide an opportunity to network with individuals in the field.

Events are open to all and are usually held at Sussex Education Centre, Mill View Hospital and Hove Polyclinic site, Nevill Avenue, Hove BN3 7HZ, from 9:15am to 5pm. Please check with smc@sussexpartnership.nhs.uk for specific details.

See the events listing and download the flyer and booking forms below for more details and to apply for a place:

  • SMC Masterclass event booking form 2018_2019
  • Mindfulness-Based Compassionate Living Foundation Course 2018 application form

Send completed booking forms to smc@sussexpartnership.nhs.uk
