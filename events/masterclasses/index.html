Events to support the development of MBCT teachers and people interested in mindfulness. Led by national and internationally renown speakers, as well as local mindfulness teachers with particular areas of expertise. They present cutting edge contemporary mindfulness-oriented approaches in various different contexts.

Workshops will include a mixture of teaching, discussion and experiential practice and provide an opportunity to network with individuals in the field.

Events are open to all and are held at Sussex Education Centre, Mill View Hospital and Hove Polyclinic site, Nevill Avenue, Hove BN3 7HZ, from 9:15am to 5pm. Please check with smc@sussexpartnership.nhs.uk for specific details.
