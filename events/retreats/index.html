Day retreats or all days are held on Saturdays from 10am to 4pm at Sussex Education Centre, Nevill Avenue, Hove, BN3 7HZ on the Mill View hospital and Hove Polyclinic site.  If you have attended an 8 week MBCT or MBSR course and are a current or previous Sussex Partnership service user, current staff member or current SMC trainee, please just turn up - no booking or payment is required. Please bring your own lunch, and also mat, cushion/stool and blanket if possible as there are only a few available.
