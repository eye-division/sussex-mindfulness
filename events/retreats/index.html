If you would like to take part, please email [smc@sussexpartnership.nhs.uk](mailto:smc@sussexpartnership.nhs.uk) and ask for the joining link and password.  Please do not share it with anyone but direct any other interested people you may know to email SMC. Please aim to join on 14th Nov by 9.50-9.55am for a prompt 10am start, finishing at 4pm.  Please arrange to stay for the whole day.  There will be breaks in the day but if you choose to come, in order to support the cohesion of the whole group practising together, we ask that you commit to attending for the whole of the scheduled time.  Please consider in advance what will best support you during the day.  We will be aiming to maintain silence through the day (from 10 until just before 4pm including during breaks and lunch) and refraining as far as possible from other distractions that are likely to interrupt the thread of your presence of mind - for example, we strongly suggest you turn off phones and other online activities, avoid reading and writing anything more than very brief notes, and let anyone you may live with or who may contact you know what you are doing, so for the 6 hours of this event, you minimise interactions as far as you can.   ---

Due to Covid-19 social distancing rules, day retreats (or mindfulness 'all days') are currently held on online. They run from 10am to 4pm, but we ask people to join from 9.50-9.55 for a 10am prompt start. 

If you have attended an 8-week MBCT or MBSR course and are a current or previous Sussex Partnership service user, current staff member or current Sussex Mindfulness Centre trainee, you are welcome to participate. No payment is required. In order to book a place and receive joining details, please email smc@sussexpartnership.nhs.uk

You'll need to attend for the whole day; it's not possible to only come for a portion of the day - thank you for your co-operation.
