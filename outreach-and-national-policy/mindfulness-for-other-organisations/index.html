Sussex Mindfulness Centre also provides mindfulness services to other organisations. With our wide network of trained and experienced teachers, trainers and researchers, we can offer you:

  • mindfulness groups for your staff
  • day or week long retreats
  • mindfulness-based supervision or consultation
  • mindfulness teacher training
  • support and guidance with mindfulness research

All of this bespoke to suit your service or organisation. SMC takes quality and safety very seriously as evidenced by all of our teachers meeting national standards outlined in the Good Practice Guidelines. With our integration of mindfulness practice and research expertise, we support the provision of mindfulness work with high quality evaluation or research, whether you want to conduct an audit or a Randomised Control Trial. If you want to find out more please feel free to contact us.   

We have recently provided a programme of mindfulness groups for staff at the Office for National Statistics and at Western Sussex Hospital NHS Foundation Trust. This is what they said:

Working with SMC to deliver Mindfulness to ONS staff has been very positive and a pleasure.  The training has been  well received and we are looking forward to seeing the results from the RCT, which will give us scientific evidence of its effectiveness.
(Dr Louisa Blackwell, Demographic Methods Centre, Methodology Group, Office for National Statistics)

The detailed evaluation data from the 8 week programmes demonstrates the positive impact for individuals in attending the programme. SPT has worked flexibly with the Trust to support us in delivering mindfulness as part of our H&W programme for staff.
(Jo Fanning, Head of Employee Relations, Western Sussex Hospitals NHS Foundation Trust)
