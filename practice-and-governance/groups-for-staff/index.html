We have provided mindfulness groups for Sussex Partnership staff for about 15 years.

Download: Read about what's involved in a staff mindfulness group.

Currently there is only one group in 2018 that is available for staff. This is a Mindful Self-Compassion group which is a 9 week mindfulness group with a strong emphasis on kindness and compassion.  More information about this style of group is available from https://centerformsc.org/. If you would like to sign up for it, please complete this form: Staff Course Application and send it to smc@sssexpartnership.nhs.uk.

Other mindfulness groups for staff that are underway are part of a regional project funded by Health Education England Kent Surrey Sussex (HEE KSS) which is evaluating mindfulness groups for NHS staff across the region. 

More information about these mindfulness groups can be found at bit.ly/2018HEEKSScourses. You can also email the project team at: HEEKSSWellbeingCourses@sussexpartnership.nhs.uk 

We are waiting for further information about funding before we can schedule staff mindfulness groups. As soon as we know more, the information about staff groups will be posted here. Enquiries can be directed to smc@sussexpartnership.nhs.uk.
