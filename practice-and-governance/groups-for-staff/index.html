We provide groups for Sussex Partnership staff as part of our staff well-being strategy. Bookings are taken on a first come first served basis except for staff who flag up particular support needs who will be prioritised. Read about what's involved in a staff mindfulness group.

Please see the staff group sessions below. If you would like to apply to join a group, please download and complete this short form, which also asks you to select your preference for a group, and email the form to smc@sussexpartnerhsip.nhs.uk.  

For all enquiries about staff mindfulness groups, please email smc@sussexpartnerhsip.nhs.uk.
