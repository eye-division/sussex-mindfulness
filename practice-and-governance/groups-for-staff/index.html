We have provided mindfulness groups for Sussex Partnership staff as part of our staff well-being strategy for a number of years.

Download: Read about what's involved in a staff mindfulness group.

From the Autumn 2017 through to Spring/Summer 2018 we are taking part in a regional project funded by Health Education England Kent Surrey Sussex (HEE KSS) which is evaluating mindfulness groups for NHS staff across the region. During this time, staff mindfulness groups will be running as part of this project.

Bookings for the staff mindfulness groups are taken on a first come first served basis. If you would like to learn more and to find out how to join a mindfulness group please visit bit.ly/2018HEEKSScourses

You can also email the project team at:  HEEKSSWellbeingCourses@sussexpartnership.nhs.uk
