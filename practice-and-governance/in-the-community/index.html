Potential participants looking for MBCT groups in the community (ie, outside the NHS) can search for local teachers on Be Mindful where those advertising will have completed certain minimum standards in training and experience. Anyone seeking a MBCT group should assure themselves the person teaching the group meets the national good practice guidelines.
