Potential participants looking for mindfulness groups in the community (ie, outside the NHS) can search for local teachers on the British Association of Mindfulness-Based Approaches (BAMBA)'s Register at: https://bamba.org.uk/ which lists teachers who meet the minimum standards outlined in the national Good Practice Guidelines.
