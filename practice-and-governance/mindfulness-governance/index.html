Our training organisation is a participating member of the UK Network of Mindfulness-based Teacher Training Organisations. Our teachers adhere to both the Good Practice Guidelines for teachers as well as our own additional governance criteria that all teachers of eight-week MBCT groups in the Trust must meet.

The good practice guidelines are here UK Good Practice Guidelines for Teachers For clarification of terms in the good practice guidelines, please see this: FAQs for the GPC Our additional Sussex Partnership criteria for people running eight week MBCT courses are: Governance arrangements for teaching Mindfulness Based Cognitive Therapy (MBCT) in Sussex Partnership NHS Foundation Trust Governance for staff using brief and adapted (non MBSR/MBCT) mindfulness interventions Trust Governanace for non-eight week Mindfulness Our mindfulness supervisors adhere to our governance criteria for mindfulness supervisors: Governance criteria for mindfulness supervisors Our trainers adhere to the national good practice guidelines for trainers of mindfulness-based teachers: UK good practice guidelines for trainers
