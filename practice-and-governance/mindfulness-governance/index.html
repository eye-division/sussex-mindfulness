Our training organisation is a participating member of the UK Network of Mindfulness-based Teacher Training Organisations. Our teachers adhere to both the Good Practice Guidelines for teachers as well as our own additional governance criteria that all teachers of eight-week MBCT groups in the Trust must meet.
