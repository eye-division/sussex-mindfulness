There is plenty of research activity in the Sussex Mindfulness Centre, including funded and unfunded studies. Click on the links below to find out details of some of our current, funded research studies.

Dr Clara Strauss and Dr Kate Cavanagh have been awarded a grant to convene a Clinical Research Group (CRG) on the topic of “Mindfulness based interventions in primary health care” as part of the national Mental Health Research Network. The aim of the CRGs is to gather together a set of collaborators (academics, clinicians and public and patient involvement) to identify key research priorities for a specific topic and develop proposals for funding through the NIHR. CRGs run for two years and expected to submit at least two grant proposals.
