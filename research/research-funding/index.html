There is plenty of research activity in the Sussex Mindfulness Centre, including funded and unfunded studies. Read details of some of our current, funded research studies below.

  • Clara Strauss (CI), Kate Cavanagh, Fergal Jones, Richard de Visser, Laura Lea, Rebecca Crane, Glenys Parry, Sarah Byford, Michael Barkham, Claire Rosten, Anna-Marie Jones (2016): Grant from Research for Patient Benefit (RfPB) for a randomised controlled trial comparing supported Mindfulness-Based Cognitive Therapy self-help to supported Cognitive Behaviour Therapy self-help for adults experiencing depression.
  • Clara Strauss (CI), Jenny Gu, Adrian Whittington, Cavita Chapman (2017). Funding from Health Education England Kent Surrey Sussex to evaluate wellbeing interventions (including MBCT) for NHS mental health staff within a randomised controlled trial.
  • Clara Strauss (CI), Kate Cavanagh: Funding from Headspace to evaluate the feasibility of a mindfulness app for people experiencing mild-moderate depression using IAPT services.
  • Clara Strauss & Kate Cavanagh (2014): Award from UK Mental Health Research Network (MHRN) to set up and support a Clinical Research Group in Low-Intensity Mindfulness-Based Interventions in Primary Care and Public Health.
  • Clara Strauss (CI), Claire Rosten, Laura Lea, Anna-Marie Jones, Mark Hayward & Elizabeth Forester (2013): National Institute of Health Research (NIHR) Research for Patient Benefit (RfPB) award for an RCT to evaluate if a mindfulness-based approach enhances outcomes for exposure and response prevention for people diagnosed with obsessive compulsive disorder.
  • Clara Strauss (CI), Kate Cavanagh & Fergal Jones (2013): Funding for an RCT of a self-help mindfulness-based intervention for NHS staff. Funding awarded by Sussex Partnership NHS Foundation Trust.
  • Clara Strauss (CI), Kate Cavanagh & Fergal Jones (2013): Fundig for an RCT of a self-help mindfulness-based intervention for depression. Grant awarded by Sussex Partnership NHS Foundation Trust own-account competition.
  • Clara Strauss & Paul Chadwick (CIs) & Mark Hayward (2010): Funding to conduct an RCT of a group mindfulness-based therapy for chronic depression. Grant awarded by Sussex Partnership NHS Foundation Trust own-account competition.
  • Paul Chadwick (CI), Mark Hayward, Clara Strauss, Claire Rosten, Anna-Marie Jones & Laura Dannahy (2011): Funding to conduct an RCT of Mindfulness for Voices (M4V) groups. Grant awarded by the National Institute of Health Research Research for Patient Benefit funding stream.
